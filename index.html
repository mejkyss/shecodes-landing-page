WHY BERGEN?

Bergen, the city nestled among seven mountains

Legend has it that Bergen, with its seven majestic mountains, is a city steeped in the enchanting tales of Huldras and the mysteries of the sea. Nestled along Norway's rugged coastline, this picturesque port has been a source of inspiration for centuries. Its colorful Hanseatic buildings and rich maritime history have captured the imaginations of visitors and locals alike. Bergen's legends continue to unfold, inviting travelers to explore its captivating landscapes and uncover the stories hidden within its ancient streets.
