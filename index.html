WHY BERGEN?

Bergen, the city nestled among seven mountains

Legend has it that Bergen, with its seven majestic mountains, is a city steeped in the enchanting tales of Huldras and the mysteries of the sea. Nestled along Norway's rugged coastline, this picturesque port has been a source of inspiration for centuries. Its colorful Hanseatic buildings and rich maritime history have captured the imaginations of visitors and locals alike. Bergen's legends continue to unfold, inviting travelers to explore its captivating landscapes and uncover the stories hidden within its ancient streets.

Beyond the legends, Bergen is a vibrant modern city with a unique blend of history and contemporary culture. Its bustling fish market, Bryggen Wharf's UNESCO-listed wooden houses, and the panoramic views from Mount Fløyen showcase its charm and beauty. Visitors can also delve into its thriving arts scene, featuring museums, galleries, and a lively music and festival calendar. Bergen's warm and welcoming atmosphere makes it not only a place of legends but a destination that leaves a lasting impression on all who venture to its shores.

A quaint fishing village with picturesque harbors and a cute little café.
